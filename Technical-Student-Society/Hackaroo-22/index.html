A Hackathon for all

Hackroo’22 is a 24-hour-long beginner-friendly hackathon. This hackathon features multiple problem statements which can be solved by students who have little coding experience. We also have some technical twists for the coding geeks. All day long we are going to have some fun activities and jamming sessions besides coding. Free food for all the participants and exciting Perks for the contest winners. So gear up for the most exciting hackathon ever!

Problem Statements

Android Development

Build an app or a game using the Android tech-stack.

Web Development

Build any Website/Web app with completely working Front-end and Back-end.

ML/ AI

Build a project based on Machine Learning/Artificial Intelligence

Open Innovation

Freshers/programming beginners can build a project based on any technology of their choice.

Perks

Computer Society of India (CSI) is one of the most prominent and renowned computer society of India. As part of your participation perks you will get the complementary 1 Year membership of CSI. CSI has a publication system where students can contribute their research work as well as learn from other researchers.

Get a chance to meet people who expertise in the field of Technology, Higher Education, Immigration and many more. Plan your future with the guidance of experts. Build up a good level of connections with students as well as seniors of the department. Also meet students from different universities.

Get a chance to win surprise gifts in differ fun activities in throughout the event. A lot of fun games, quizzes and many more exciting gifts await you.
